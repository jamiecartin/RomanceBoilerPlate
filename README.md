An interactive Java-based RPG that tells the story of your relationship and culminates in a marriage proposal. Created with love for that special someone.

## Features ✨
- Personalized narrative journey through your relationship milestones
- Multiple choice interactions based on real memories
- Romantic ASCII art displays
- Customizable chapters and dialogue
- Progressive story building leading to the big question

## Requirements 📋
- Java JDK 11 or newer
- Text editor for personalization
- Your unique love story 💖
